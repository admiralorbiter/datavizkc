Competition Guidelines

Competition Divisions

Follow your Passion:
  • This is an open division where participants will create a visualization based on a topic of their choice
  • Teams are on their own to select dataset to use
  • Division is for high school only!
Community Impact: Greater Kansas City Region
  • Teams will create a visualization that is based on datasets that are focused on community impact
  • Teams will can use datasets provided by the competition organizers
  • This division is for middle school and high school

Contest Eligibility

To be eligible for the competition, you must be a student or a teacher at the middle or high school level from a school district that partners with PREP-KC. Teams of teachers and/or students are eligible to compete under the following guidelines:

  • Maximum of 5 individuals on a team
  • Teams including teachers can have up to two teachers on the team.
    • Examples:
    • 1 teacher & (2-4) students
    • 2 teachers & (2-3) students
  • A team of only students is also allowed.

Contest Window

  • BEGINS: September 28th 2022 (Launch/info session)
  • ENDS: March 31st 2023 (Final day to submit visualization)
  • Teams are allowed to submit only 1 entry per division
  • Participants can enter the competition anytime during the competition window

Contest Prizes!

All eligible teams will compete for a $2,500 prize pool. Look for more details after the big launch!
