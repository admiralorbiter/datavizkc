Competition Guidelines

Contest Eligibility

To be eligible for the competition, you must be a student or a teacher at the middle or high school level from a school district that partners with PREP-KC. Teams of teachers and/or students are eligible to compete under the following guidelines: Minimum of 3 individuals on a team.

  • Every team must have at least two students.
  • Maximum of 5 individuals on a team
  • Teams including teachers can have up to two teachers on the team.
    • Examples:
    • 1 teacher & (2-4) students
    • 2 teachers & (2-3) students
  • A team of only students is also allowed.

Contest Window

  • BEGINS: March 3rd 2022 (Launch/info session)
  • ENDS: May 2nd 2022 (Final day to submit visualization)
  • Teams are allowed to submit only 1 entry.
  • Participants can enter the competition anytime during the competition window

Contest Prizes!

All eligible teams will compete for a $2,500 prize pool. Look for more details after the big launch!
