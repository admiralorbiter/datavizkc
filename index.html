DataViz PREP-KC

2nd Annual DataViz PREP-KC Competition

The competition invites teams of high school teachers and students from PREP-KC's partner urban districts to work together to create compelling data visualizations. This year we will have two division with the open division following last years theme, FOLLOW YOUR PASSION, and Greater Kansas City Region community impact theme where teams will be asked to create a visualization that will help PREP-KC better understand the impact of data in their community.
